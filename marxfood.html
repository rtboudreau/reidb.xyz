ecommerce website

Marx Foods

MarxFood.com is Wordpress site comprised of 10 years worth of recipes, tutorials, user contests and other SEO-rich pages and posts. When it became time to redevelop and modernize the site in 2017 it was important for us to stick to with the Wordpress CMS in order to maintain nearly a decade's worth of work and effort.

I was tasked with building a site that reflected the ecommerce site – MarxFoods.com – so that end users could seamlessly navigate from recipes to shopping pages and back again. With over 25,000 unique users per month across various screen sizes, it was also crucial for the site to be fully responsive with a quick load time. According to Pingdom, the current load time is routinely below 1.2 seconds, which is an accomplishment that I’m proud of for a Wordpress platform that is often considered “bloated”.

As a result of the redevelopment we have seen a 40% increase in page views / time on page and a revenue increase of roughly 30% from users that originated from the recipe blog.
