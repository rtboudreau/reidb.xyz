Hi, I'm Reid Boudreau and I am a web developer with over 8 years of experience. I excel at responsive development, cross browser compatibility, and crafting beautiful web pages with compelling content and seamless usability.

Originally from a creative and marketing background I kept finding myself drawn to projects that relied on coding. Through years or practice, online tutorials, books, and a sustained inquiry I’ve grown into a proficient and efficient developer.

I have worked across a diversity of content management systems and frameworks including: NetSuite SCA, Bootstrap, Backbone.js, Drupal, and WordPress. Recently I’ve tasked myself with learning Ruby/Ruby on Rails and have my first application, To-Dos, hosted on Heroku. I approach each project with thourough consideration in order to produce reusable, scalable, coherent, and clean code.

I routinely dive headfirst into new languages and frameworks looking for ways to grow, to do things differently, and to improve as a developer. Trends in web development are fleeting - what’s “hot” today may not be tomorrow - and so I find myself constantly moving and improving.

I reside in the Seattle area with my wife and son. I enjoy cycling, eating oysters, trips to the peninsula, spiral cut hot-dogs, and now thanks to my toddler: throwing rocks into The Sound.

Please do not hesitate to contact me if there are any project that I can help you with.
