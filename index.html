I'm driven by the desire of doing things better, more efficently, differently.

A web developer with 10+ years of experience I excel at responsive development, cross browser compatibility, and crafting beautiful web pages with compelling content and seamless usability. I approach each project with thorough consideration in order to produce reusable, scalable, coherent, and clean code.

Originally from a creative and marketing background I routinely found myself drawn to coding based projects. Through years or practice, online tutorials, books, the occasional class, and a sustained inquiry I’ve grown into a proficient and efficient developer.

I have worked across a diversity of content management systems and frameworks including: NetSuite SCA, Bootstrap, Backbone.js, Drupal, and WordPress. Recently I’ve tasked myself with learning Ruby on Rails and have my first application, To-Dos, hosted on Heroku.

I routinely dive headfirst into new languages and frameworks looking for ways to grow, to do things differently, and to improve as a developer. Trends in web development are fleeting - what’s “hot” today may not be tomorrow - and so I find myself constantly moving and improving.

I live in the Seattle area with my wife and son. I enjoy cycling, eating oysters, trips to the peninsula, spiral cut hot-dogs, and a seeemingly endless supply of home improvement projects.

Please do not hesitate to contact me in regard to job openings or projects that I can be of assistance on.
