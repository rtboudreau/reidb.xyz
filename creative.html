Creative

Ferrytales.xyz is a minimally designed, text forward, Wordpress site that I built as a passion project. The impetus for the project was to have a platform for me, and potentially other ferry commuter, to publish short stories that were written during the daily passage on the Washington State Ferries from Bainbridge Island to Seattle.

My Role

  • Developing all PHP, CSS, and JavaScript assets for the project.
  • Assisting design team with UX and layout.
  • Creating, resizing, and optimizing images and graphics.
  • Implementing Google Tag Manager.
  • General updating, maintenance, and troubleshooting.
