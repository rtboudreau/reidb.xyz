MarxImports.com

Marx Imports is world class importer of specialty meats with an acute focus on animal welfare. Ranging from New Zealand grass-fed beef to the highly sought after Spanish Iberico pork, their products occupy the shelves of national retailers, appear on the menus of Michelin starred restaurants, and comprise the ingredients in major specialty food products.

rief

Over the past decade Marx Imports' market reach and visibility experienced rapid growth and they needed a website that better reflected their position in the market. To deliver this product I worked closely with the creative team to to ensure that brand guidelines and the spec sheet were followed and implemented as intended. Utilizing PHP as well as Bootstrap I was able to deliver an extensive static site that is full responsive and compatible across browsers.

Following the redevelopment of Marx Imports there has been a 37% growth in users and a 27% growth in organic search traffic.

My Role

  • Developing all HTML, CSS, and JavaScript assets for the project.
  • Assisting design team with UX and layout.
  • Creating, resizing, and optimizing images and graphics.
  • Implementing Google Tag Manager.
  • General updating, maintenance, and troubleshooting.
