MarxFoods.com

Marxfoods.com is an e-commerce site founded in 2007 that aims to bring restaurant quality food to home & professional chefs alike. I was brought onboard during the 6th iteration of the site and for the past three years I was tasked with developing, maintaining, and growing the 7th iteration, which introduced responsive design and a mobile first development process.

Iss
ue

Marx Foods’ product catalog had grown to over 1,500 products and the site was attracting millions of annual visitors. Since the previous iteration in 2014 we, like everyone else, experienced a growing percentage of users reaching the site on mobile devices & tablets. The challenge was to use as many existing assets as possible while developing a responsive site, retaining our unique brand, and better serving our new customers while continuing to provide a welcoming experience to our existing customers.

Through steady A/B testing, user surveys, and analyzing user flows, the site has received a steady stream of improvements resulting in measured success for both the company and customers. Since the deployment of the 7th iteration of Marx Foods organic search traffic has increased by 36%, total online transaction are up 53%, and the average order value has risen by 24%.
